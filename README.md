###  Telco Customer Churn Prediction (Classification)
*An end-to-end project to identify at-risk customers and provide actionable business insights.*

#### **Technical Deep Dive:**
* **Advanced Cleaning:** Handled numerical conversion for `TotalCharges` and managed missing values.
* **Feature Encoding:** Implemented a comprehensive strategy using **Manual Mapping** for binary features and **One-Hot Encoding** for multi-class categories.
* **Model Strategy:** Focused on **Logistic Regression** with `class_weight='balanced'` to handle the inherent imbalance in churn data.
* **Feature Selection:** Used **Chi-Square (SelectKBest)** to identify the top 22 features that most impact customer decisions.
* **Threshold Tuning (Business Decision):** Instead of just taking the default 0.5, I experimented with different **Probability Thresholds (0.3 to 0.4)** to maximize **Recall (reaching 93-94%)**. This ensures the business captures almost all customers likely to leave.

#### **Key Business Insights:**
* **Tenure Impact:** Customers with shorter tenure are at a much higher risk of churning.
* **Contract Type:** "Month-to-month" contracts are the strongest predictors of churn.
* **Visualization:** Created a **Correlation Heatmap**, **Confusion Matrices**, and **Probability Distribution** plots to communicate model reliability.

[ View Full Notebook on GitHub](https://github.com/AmalZeinn/WA_Fn-UseC_-Telco-Customer-Churn)
